What is homecooks?

We believe in providing the chefs in our community - individuals who have always dreamt of building their own food business or sharing their recipes with those around them - the opportunity to make an income by doing what they love! We also believe every person should have access to authentic, homemade food at an affordable price.

How it works:

Explore chefs in your neighbourhood

Get authentic, homemade food prepared locally, by approved chefs.

Place your order

Order in advance so your chef can buy fresh ingredients, and cook to order.

Get your delivery

Dishes arrive at your door refrigerated at a time that suits you. Just heat up and enjoy!

FOOD SAFETY

We take food safety extremely seriously and are committed to helping ensure that your food will always be safe to eat. All HomeCooks applicants are required to provide food safety documentation, as well as registration with their local council. If you’re unsure of the procedure - our team will be more than happy to provide you with support throughout the process.
